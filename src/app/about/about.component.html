Passion Project: GeekCon Pakistan

It really all started with a few friends who were massive fans of anime, comics, and all things geeky, just looking for a place to connect. That small idea grew into Geek Con, which has become Pakistan's biggest comic convention. For me, it's always been a passion project. The best part is seeing thousands of people come together, whether they're in full cosplay, showing off their art, or just there to soak it all in. It's about creating a space where everyone can celebrate what they love and find their community.

# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and engineering peers in Germany/EU evaluating Zain for Werkstudent, internship, and junior roles. They arrive from a CV, LinkedIn, or GitHub link, scan for credibility in seconds, and decide whether to open a repo or reach out.

## Product Purpose
Prove technical craft through the site itself. Convert profile visits into interviews by making Zain's full-stack + AI/LLM + security work legible and memorable.

## Brand Personality
Precise, engineered, approachable. A developer who ships — dark, technical surface with a warm signature (the orange→blue radial gradient) and small human touches (hidden cat easter egg).

## Anti-references
No template-portfolio look. No fake claims — every project statement must match the actual repo. No hacker-terminal cosplay.

## Design Principles
- The existing identity (near-black surface, orange #f27b01 → blue #5792bb radial gradient, dark cards) is committed brand color — refine it, don't replace it.
- Typography carries the upgrade: Archivo for display/body, Martian Mono for technical accents (typed role line, tags, dates).
- Motion is subtle and purposeful; everything respects prefers-reduced-motion.
- WCAG AA contrast floor; visible focus states; keyboard-operable nav.
